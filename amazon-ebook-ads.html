How we help

Amazon ads work best when the book is positioned first.

Ads can put your ebook in front of more readers, but the campaign still needs a strong reason to click. We connect the book's promise, audience, keywords, ad structure, and sales page so each piece supports the next.

01

Reader and keyword strategy

We map the kinds of readers most likely to care, then build keyword groups around search behavior, author comps, topic terms, and buying intent.

02

Campaign setup

We organize Sponsored Products campaigns so testing is easier to read, budgets are easier to manage, and winners are easier to scale over time.

03

Creative and conversion review

We look at the title, subtitle, cover, description, reviews, author presence, and ad copy together so the click has a clear next step.

04

Optimization rhythm

We review search terms, spend, click behavior, and sales signals to refine bids, pause weak paths, and keep the campaign focused on qualified readers.

For ebook authors

This is built for nonfiction, expertise-driven, and brand-adjacent books where the ebook is part of a larger relationship with the reader. The goal is not just more traffic; it is better traffic with a clearer reason to buy.
